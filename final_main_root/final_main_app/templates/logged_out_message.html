An overview. Features based on user needs.

A social network with the aim of assisting users through peer support groups, a knowledge hub and stories. Use group and individual online chat to make support sessions more convenient. A gamified knowledge hub to encourage user participation and knowledge sharing. Sharing of user stories to motivate peers on the network into positive behavioural changes.

The motivation. See for yourself.

The motivation for this project is that there is evidence of positive outcomes for online peer support groups and that there is increasing interest from users for online peer support and mental health support. Thus users can receive coping mechanisms and mental health support online through peer support groups. Physical peer support groups are a good form of support but can be restricted to physical groups which can be limited by location and awareness of such support sessions. The internet made a lot of services more accessible including support groups.

Chat discussions. Improve your mental health.

Online group chat and individual chat features will work well, with any location and many devices it will allow users to find peers that can create peer support groups. Also, with physical peer support groups, there is no persisted knowledge sharing or search functionality for knowledge, thus a knowledge hub in the form of a Question And Answer style forum will be added

User written stories. Share knowledge and motivate others.

Then as part of the platform, users will be able to create rich content stories about their experiences in overcoming difficult social and mental challenges and coping mechanisms, and they can share these stories for their peers on the platform to consume and benefit from.
